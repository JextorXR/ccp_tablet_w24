Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a staple food made by many american households and a italtian classic. This recipe is quick and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Angel Hair Pasta
  • Red Pasta Sauce
  • Parmasean Cheese
  • Ground beef or turkey

Directions

  1. Cook meat in a skillet with oil until brown
  2. boil a pot of water and add noodles, cook for 8 minutes
  3. Strain noodles and return to stock pot
  4. Combine meat and sauce with noodles
